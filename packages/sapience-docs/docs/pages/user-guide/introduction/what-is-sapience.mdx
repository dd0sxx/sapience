### What is Sapience?

Sapience is a platform for open source, fully transparent prediction markets. Built fully onchain and powered by USDe, Sapience is designed to make forecasting liquid, composable, and accessible — whether you’re an individual forecaster, a builder deploying agents, or a professional trading desk.

Our developer tools are geared towards both hobbyist builders and institutional participants, providing a seamless path to spin up bots, strategies, and integrations.

Read more on our blog: [Introducing Sapience](https://blog.sapience.xyz/p/introducing-sapience)
