### Fees & Incentives

- **Trading Fees** – Low, transparent fees apply to each trade.
- **LP Rewards** – LPs earn a share of fees plus the USDe reward rate.
- **Incentives for Builders** – Developers can integrate bots, agents, and vaults to capture additional upside.
- **Alignment** – Fee structures are designed to reward active participation, tight spreads, and broad market coverage.
